QIIME 2™ is a microbiome multi-omics bioinformatics and data science platform that is extensible, free, open source, and community developed and supported.

Code of Conduct » Citing QIIME 2 » Learn more »

Integrated provenance tracking ensures bioinformatics reproducibility and even allows you to "replay" your analysis steps on the same or new data — no more guesswork on what commands were run!

Beautiful, interactive visualizations provide new perspectives and help translate your data into knowledge.

Easily share your results with your team or the world! Viewers don't need to have QIIME 2 installed.

QIIME 2 plugins put your favorite microbiome methods all in one place, and enable bioinformatics developers to rapidly share new methods with QIIME 2 users.

QIIME 2's different interfaces allow you to work in the environment that you're most productive with.

command line interface the high-performance computing (HPC) user or power user's interface

Python 3 API the data scientist or pipeline developer's interface

q2galaxy the fully graphical interface, for biologists and clinicians who don't want to code

Citing QIIME 2

QIIME 2 plugins frequently utilize other software packages that must be cited in addition to QIIME 2 itself. Run qiime tools citations on an Artifact or Visualization to discover all of the citations relevant to the creation of that result. Lists of citations are provided by https://view.qiime2.org as well. To view the list of citations for a QIIME 2 Artifact or Visualization, load your result at https://view.qiime2.org and then navigate to the "Details" tab.
